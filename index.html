About

Tantragyan - National Level Project Competition.


Lokmanya Tilak College of engineering is affiliated to Mumbai University and is striving hard for academic excellence of the student’s community in the country.

In this endeavor to encourage the hidden talent in the diploma and degree students of engineering, Department of Electrical Engineering, Student council of electrical Engineering (SCEE) and IEEE/LTCE organized the national level project competition named as “TANTRAGYAN”. This national level project competition was organized in Lokmanya Tilak College of Engineering for the last four year under the guidance of Prof (Dr). C.M. Wankhade . In this event we provided an opportunity for the students to explore their creative instincts in the technical field and showcase their talents in the form of innovative projects. This event also enhanced practical knowledge, gave chance to modify and develop the existing technologies to ensure its improvement provide contribution for better tomorrow.

LOKMANYA TILAK COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING


Lokmanya Tilak College of Engineering (LTCOE), established in 1994, is a premier regional engineering college in Navi Mumbai at Koparkhairane.

The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and is recognized by the government of Maharashtra. The Directorate of Technical Education, Government of Maharashtra has granted an "A" ranking to the college. College is active in several curricular & non- curricular activities. This helps in developing overall personality of future engineers. The college encourages & fosters team spirit and a culture of learning through unique events. The main aim of the college is to impart practical on road knowledge to our students. The college is affiliated to Mumbai University strategically situated in the vicinity of Dhirubhai Ambani Knowledge City, Siemens, CG, BB, Millenium Business Park and Thane Belapur industrial belt.
